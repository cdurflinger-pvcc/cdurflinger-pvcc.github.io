Natural language giving computer the ability to understand text or spoken words
Formal language set of strings of symbols drawn from a finite alphabet
Machine independence software that is not dependent on the properties of a particular machine

Object-oriented programming (OOP) an approach to systems development that focuses on objects and the way that they interac
